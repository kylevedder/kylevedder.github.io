I am a PhD student under Eric Eaton in the Lifelong Machine Learning Lab in GRASP at the University of Pennsylvania. My work focuses on long-term autonomy for service robots, building towards care robots for the elderly and disabled.

In undergrad I worked under Joydeep Biswas in the Autonomous Mobile Robotics Lab on the RoboCup SSL team UMass Minutebots where I worked on the core infrastructure and the low level safety system and did research in single-agent and multi-agent path finding.

I have prior industry experience as an intern at Google, Amazon Lab126, and an aquired startup.
